#OFTD - Outfit Of The Day

Michelle & The Pixies have decided to use Firebase as the backend for this project.

REQUIREMENTS:
	- Settings page
	- Push notification of what I should wear
	- Cool assets!
	- User defaults (save settings)

OPTIONALS:
	- Graph: Facebook Analytics API
	- Search within graph
	- History of what I wore
	- Cool outfit combo to save

OpenWeatherMap API: Current Weather endpoint by geographic coordinates
Model class: TodayOutfit (what to wear, temperature, etc.)
